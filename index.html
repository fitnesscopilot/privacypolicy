2. Information we collect

2.1 Account and identity data

When you create or use an account, we may collect:

  • Name and display name
  • Email address and email verification status
  • Country / region
  • Authentication identifiers (such as Firebase UID, provider IDs, tokens)
  • Password hashes if you register with email/password (we never store your plain password)
  • Third‑party sign‑in information (e.g., Google account basic profile, as provided by the identity provider)

2.2 Subscription and payment data

We use Razorpay or other payment processors to handle payments. We may process:

  • Subscription plan, status, and renewal dates
  • Purchase history, order ID, amount, currency, and timestamps
  • Limited billing metadata returned by the payment processor

We do not store full payment instrument details such as full card numbers, CVV, or full UPI details. Those are collected and processed by Razorpay or the relevant payment provider under their own privacy policies.

2.3 Workout, nutrition, and app content data

To provide coaching features, we store data you enter or generate in the app, including:

  • Workout plans, exercises, sets, reps, weights, rest times, and logs
  • Diet plans, meal logs, food items, quantities, macros, preferences, and exclusions
  • Progressive overload settings, consistency scores, and habit metrics
  • AI conversations and prompts you exchange with the in‑app coach
  • Notes you add, custom foods or exercises you create, and other content you voluntarily provide

Some of this content may reveal information about your health, habits, and lifestyle. We treat it as sensitive and use it only to provide the Service and improve your experience, not for advertising based on health profiles.

2.4 Device and technical data

When you use the Service, we automatically collect certain technical information, such as:

  • Device identifiers (for example, device model, OS version, language, app version)
  • IP address and approximate location (city/region) derived from IP
  • Device settings and configuration (such as time zone)
  • Log data about how you interact with the app (screens viewed, features used, taps and navigation events)
  • Crash logs, performance data, and diagnostics

2.5 Cookies and local storage

We may use:

  • Cookies, where applicable (for example if you use a web version)
  • Local storage on your device (for example, secure storage, AsyncStorage)

These are used for:

  • Authentication and session management
  • Caching and performance
  • Security protections (for example, detecting abuse)
  • Remembering preferences (theme, hints, onboarding state)
  • Measuring anonymous usage metrics and in‑app events

2.6 Support and communication data

If you contact us, we may collect:

  • Your email address and other contact details
  • Support requests and content of your communications
  • Technical information related to the issue (logs, device info)

3. How we obtain your information

We collect information:

  • Directly from you when you sign up, log workouts, interact with the coach, or contact support
  • Automatically when you use the Service, through SDKs and analytics tools
  • From third parties when you authorize them (e.g., Google sign‑in, payment providers)

You may choose not to provide certain information, but it may limit your ability to use some features.

4. Legal bases for processing (where applicable)

Where data protection law (such as the GDPR) applies, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract: To create and manage your account, provide coaching, sync your data, and deliver subscriptions.
  • Legitimate interests: To maintain security, fix bugs, prevent abuse, understand app usage, and improve features in a way that respects your privacy.
  • Consent: For optional analytics, certain notifications, and marketing communications, where required by law.
  • Legal obligations: To comply with tax, accounting, fraud prevention, and other legal requirements.

5. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  1. Provide and operate the Service
    Authenticate you and manage sessions; store and sync your workouts, diet plans, and logs across devices; render personalized suggestions, plans, and educational content; maintain progressive overload tracking and consistency metrics.
  2. Process payments and manage subscriptions
    Verify purchases and entitlements; handle free trials, renewals, upgrades, and cancellations; detect and prevent fraudulent transactions.
  3. Maintain security and prevent abuse
    Monitor authentication activity and misuse; detect spam, abuse, or unauthorized access attempts; protect the integrity of our systems and other users.
  4. Improve and develop the Service
    Diagnose crashes and performance problems; analyze aggregate usage trends; test new features and UX changes.
  5. Communicate with you
    Send service messages; respond to support requests; send optional tips or product updates where permitted (you may opt out at any time).
  6. Comply with legal obligations and enforce our rights
    Maintain records for accounting and tax; respond to lawful requests; enforce our Terms & Conditions and protect our rights and those of our users.

We do not sell your personal data.
